ABOUT ME

My Name is Ariel Daud I am an Instagram Filter Creator. I started making filters on Instagram in September 2019. I can make various kinds of Instagram filters, for example, Game Filters, Quiz Filters, Make up Filters, Color Preset / LUT Filters, Typography Filters, Music Filters, Vintage Camera Filters.
